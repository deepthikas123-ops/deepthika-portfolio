Philosophy

How I
think about
design

"Great products are built when design leads and engineering shapes them into reality — not the other way around."

01
Empathy First
Every decision begins by understanding the person who will use it — their context, their friction, their joy.
02
Form Follows Feeling
Aesthetics aren't decoration. The way something looks shapes how it's trusted, used, and remembered.
03
Prototype to Think
Ideas crystallise through making. A rough prototype reveals more than a perfect spec document ever could.
04
Engineer the Craft
Understanding how things are built makes designs more honest, feasible, and ultimately more impactful.
